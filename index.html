1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects – How does GDPR impact digital projects?

GDPR is the replacement for the 1995 Data Protection Directive. GDPR is to protect individuals right to access personal data they may have stored about them. It affects every company that process large amounts of data. The constraints will be that consent will have to be given by the consumer, and if there are any changes the consumers will have to be notified which is time consuming. The companies will have to implement tools to allow the consumer to access data and delete if they wish. e.g Facebook. If the consumer withholds data it could have a major impact on companies that function on the retention of consumer data. The legal guideline follows a certain criteria such as storage, access, amendment and privacy. These principles should lie at the heart of your approach to processing personal data.
